== Description ==

Carpenter Shop serves as your digital workshop, providing a specialized online space to showcase your carpentry skills and creations. It's a special design that helps you create an online space for showcasing and promoting your carpentry services or products. Think of it as a digital storefront and workshop combined. With this theme, you can easily display your carpentry projects, whether it's custom furniture, renovations, or other woodworking creations. It's perfect for carpenters, craftsmen, or anyone involved in woodworking who wants to showcase their skills and attract potential clients. The theme is designed to be user-friendly, so you don't need to be a tech expert to use it. It offers an intuitive setup, allowing you to upload images of your completed projects, share information about your services, and even interact with potential customers. Visual appeal is a key feature â€“ the theme comes with a clean and stylish layout to showcase your carpentry work in the best possible light. It's like creating an online portfolio that acts as a digital resume for your carpentry skills. The Carpenter Shop WordPress theme is a tool that helps you reach a broader audience and establish your carpentry brand online. Whether you're a seasoned carpenter or just starting your woodworking journey, this theme provides a simple and effective way to share your passion and craftsmanship with the world.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
